Pasta with Pesto

Pasta (2 cups), Olive Oil (1 tbsp), Salt (1 tsp), Pesto (2 tbsp)

Bring a large pot of water to the boil. Add oil and salt. Add pasta and boil until al dente (10-12 mins). Drain pasta. Add pesto and stir.
